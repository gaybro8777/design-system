Acceptable Formats

Uploaded resumes must be less than 3MB and in one of the following document formats: GIF, JPG, JPEG, PNG, RTF, PDF or Word (DOC or DOCX).

Please Note:

  • Certain agencies only accept resumes created through the USAJOBS Resume Builder.
  • Please do not include photos of yourself or sensitive information in your resume as this can make your application unacceptable.
